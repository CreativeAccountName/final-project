Average Color Finder

This tool exists to let users easily find the average color value across their favorite movies' official poster, or across an image uploaded by the user.

To use this tool, either type a movie title into the searchbox below or upload an image file from your device.
Note: Large files may take a long time to load.

    When Using the Search feature, please note:
  • Only the first search result is used. This may lead to images unrelated to your intended film loading into view, depending on the search result.
  • Gibberish, misspelled titles, or titles not listed in the database may return an unrelated image or produce no results.
